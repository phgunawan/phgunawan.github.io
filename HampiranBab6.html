Langkah 3. Membangun model regresi linier sederhana¶

Menentukan matriks $\textbf{A}$ dan $\mathbf{Y}$

Matriks $A$ \begin{equation*} \textbf{A}=\left[\begin{matrix} 1 & x_{11} & x_{12} & \cdots & x_{1n}\\ 1 & x_{21} & x_{22} & \cdots & x_{2n}\\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots\\ 1 & x_{m1} & x_{m2} & \cdots & x_{mn} \end{matrix}\right]_{(m\times n+1)}, \end{equation*}

vektor $y$ \begin{equation*} \textbf{Y}=\left[\begin{matrix} y_{11} & \cdots & y_{1p}\\ y_{21} & \cdots & y_{2p}\\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ y_{m1} & \cdots & y_{mp} \end{matrix}\right]_{(m\times p)}. \end{equation*}

Mencari koefisien $C$

Permasalahan Least Squared, \begin{equation*} \displaystyle \min_{\mathbf{C} \in \mathbb{R}^{(n+1) \times p}}|| \mathbf{Y} - \hat{\mathbf{Y}}||^2, \end{equation*} atau \begin{equation*} \displaystyle \min_{\mathbf{C} \in \mathbb{R}^{(n+1) \times p}}|| \mathbf{Y} - \mathbf{AC}||^2. \end{equation*} dengan $||\cdot||$ menotasikan Frobenius norm.

solusinya adalah \begin{equation*} \mathbf{C} = (\mathbf{A'A})^{-1} \mathbf{A'Y} \end{equation*}

C = np.linalg.inv(np.transpose(A)@A)@np.transpose(A)@Y

def fVolvacea(x1,x2):
  return C[0][0] + C[1][0]*x1 + C[2][0]*x2

def fAuricula(x1,x2):
  return C[0][1] + C[1][1]*x1 + C[2][1]*x2

Langkah 4. Evaluasi model¶

Hypothesis testing and Tabel ANOVA untuk multivariate linear regression

In [9]:
def ANOVATAB_MVLR(y,yhat,n,p,m):
  dfn = p*n
  dfd = p*(m-n-1)
  ybar = np.average(y)

  SSR = np.sum((yhat - ybar)@np.transpose(yhat - ybar))
  SSE = np.sum((y-yhat)@np.transpose(y-yhat))
  SST = np.sum((y-ybar)@np.transpose(y-ybar))
  MSR = SSR/dfn
  MSE = SSE/dfd
  
  Fs = MSR/MSE
  ks = 1-scipy.stats.f.cdf(Fs, dfn, dfd)
  data_table= {
    'SS': [SSR, SSE, SST], 
    'df': [dfn, dfd,m-1] ,
    'MS': [MSR, MSE,'-'],
    'Fs': [Fs, '-','-'],
    'pval': [ks, '-','-']
  }
  
  return pd.DataFrame(data_table)
